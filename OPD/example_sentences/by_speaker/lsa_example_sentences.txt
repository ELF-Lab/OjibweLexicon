Namanj igo ge-inwegwen a'aw waa-nitaa-ojibwemod, booch igo da-nisidotaagod iniw manidoon. Gii-minwendaagoziwag omaa dibikong. Baataniinad mashkiki imaa waabashkikiing. Minawaanigozi a'aw abinoojiinh ingoji izhiwinind. Iishpin wese'ang, ashi asemaa. Minawaanigwad i'iw waabanda'iding. Ingii-waabamaa baa-aabitoojiinaad.
